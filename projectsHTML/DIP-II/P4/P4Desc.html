Input: An image containing five sets of differntly-sized solid black disks randomly scattered in the image.

Task: Design appropriate hit-or-miss transform to detect the smallest and largest disks.

Steps:

  • Thresholded the input image to convert it into binary image
  • Removed noise by applying closing operation
  • Created structuring elements to detect small and large disks
  • Applied hit or miss transform using the structuring elements to get the final result
